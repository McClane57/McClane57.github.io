Bio

I am a third year PhD student at Université Grenoble Alpes supervised by Franck Iutzeler, Jérôme Malick, and Massih-Reza Amini.

I am interested in optimization algorithms for large-scale applications, more precisely in first-order ones that are focused on sparsification of communications in distributed setting.

In free time I am interested in basketball, workout, swimming, hiking, climbing. In addition, I am also a member of Russian first team in different logic puzzles and sudoku competitions.
